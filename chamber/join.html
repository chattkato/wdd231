NP Membership

  • Full business directory listing on the chamber website
  • Access to all chamber networking events
  • Quarterly newsletter subscription
  • Eligibility for community grants and partnerships
  • Dedicated support from chamber liaison officers

Annual Fee: No charge (Non-profit organisations only)

Bronze Membership

  • Business directory listing with logo and contact info
  • 2 complimentary tickets to annual chamber gala
  • Access to members-only training workshops
  • 10% discount on all chamber event registrations
  • Monthly business newsletter

Annual Fee: ₦150,000

Silver Membership

  • Everything in Bronze, plus:
  • Featured spotlight on the chamber homepage (rotating)
  • Social media promotion — 2 posts/month across our channels
  • 4 complimentary event tickets per year
  • Access to government liaison introductions
  • Priority listing in the business directory
  • 25% discount on chamber advertising packages

Annual Fee: ₦300,000

Gold Membership

  • Everything in Silver, plus:
  • Permanent featured spotlight on homepage
  • 10 complimentary tickets to all major chamber events
  • Co-branding on chamber marketing materials
  • Social media promotion — 4 posts/month
  • Speaking opportunities at chamber conferences
  • Dedicated account manager
  • VIP access to government and investor networking luncheons

Annual Fee: ₦600,000
